# Midnight Proof
---
## 01
> I exited the vessel and peered onto the canyon, noticing the landmarks and memorizing them in bouts of observation. I ran my handheld computer and it showed me paths that led to total destruction. I briefly pondered what stability that would bring to the solar system, but found none.
> ---
## 02
> This is the mission. To fly to Barabas. I read a book about it, it appears as a place of abandon. I will have to pull in mineral and asteroid to sample and set more intelligent discourses that jockey toward the computers goal. Always the computer in the right, not my intuition or the punishment of my psychology. This has always been the way it is.
> ---
## 03
> The map shows the slingshot effect from a nearby planet as the cause for the incident. I crack up at the southern voice of the mascot, but the mood quickly depresses again. I make sure the awareness systems are up then move to the coffee machine, which I try to ration using a pen and paper. I don't have a lot of it left, but it's an effective mood enhancer.
> ---
## 04
